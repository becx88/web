Department of CSE (Data Science)

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) was established in 2020 to equip students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills in the rapidly evolving fields of Data Science (DS), Big Data analytics, Advanced Databases and Cognitive Analytics. CSE (Data Science) is an upcoming, exponentially growing field which consists of a set of tools and techniques used to extract useful information from data. There is a rising demand for undergraduates in Data Science in the Global market over the last few years. B.Tech. B.Tech in CSE (Data Science) has been specifically designed to educate undergraduate students for a prospective career in upcoming data science industry. With analysis and statistical approach, data science is one of the fastest growing career opportunities in the country. The program encompass data science as an interdisciplinary, problem-solving-oriented subject that learns to apply scientific techniques to practical problems. B.Tech in CSE (DS) is best suited for students who are seeking to build a career in field of data science. The curriculum include courses on programming, algorithms, data structures, computer networks, database management, and operating systems. In addition, the program educates them on emerging areas of Data Science topics such as Machine Learning, Statistics, Optimization, Big Data Analytics, NoSQL Databases, Real time Analytics and many more. This helps the students to stand apart in the fellow graduates and grow careers in the upcoming technological era. Graduates in CSE (DS) have career prospects in a variety of industries such as Retail, Finance, E-commerce, Healthcare, IT services with job roles defined as Data Scientist, Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Data Engineer and many more. Leading giants like Amazon, Google, Facebook, Microsoft, Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, HCL Technologies are currently among the top recruiters hiring graduates with skills in AI and ML with a salary package ranging from 8 Lakhs to 40 Lakhs annually.
